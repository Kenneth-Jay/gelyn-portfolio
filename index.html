About

Hi, I’m Lyn, a UI/UX Designer focused on creating intuitive, user-centered digital experiences. I began my career in Application Support, where I worked closely with users, investigated system issues, and identified usability gaps in live applications, shaping a strong user-first mindset. Over the past few years, I’ve transitioned into UI/UX design, creating interfaces that balance usability, aesthetics, and business goals. I design thoughtful web and mobile experiences while simplifying complex user flows and improving interaction efficiency. Using tools like Figma, FigJam, Balsamiq, Adobe, Photoshop, Canva, Miro, Mural, Jira, Confluence and WebFlow.I collaborate closely with teams to transform ideas into clear, engaging, and scalable designs.
